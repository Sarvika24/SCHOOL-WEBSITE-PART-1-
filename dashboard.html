Eklavya Jagal of class VII is one more step closer to his Olympic dream. He attended 18 days long Short Track Ice Skating Skill Enhancement Training Camp held at Minsk, Belarus from September 6-23, 2019. Eklavya represented Ice Skating Association of India in Belarus Open Cup and gave the best performance under-14 category. He competed with Russian, Latvian, French and Belarusian skaters. He finished first in 1000 metres Final-B, fourth in 500 meters Final-A and fifth in 777 meters Final-A.

Ananya Srivastava (IX) and Aradhya Goel (VIII) participated in 27th State level National Children’s Science Congress held at RPM Academy, Gorakhpur from 4th December, 2019 to 6th December, 2019 during which they presented the scientific project. They were recognized as the State Child Scientist by Council of Science and Technology, U.P (Department of Science and Technology, Government of UP).

Niamat Gill, of class VIII, won the first position at the State level Science Camp organized by Vidyarthi Vigyan Manthan (VVM) held at Sarvottam International School, Greater Noida on 12th January, 2020. She won a cash prize of Rs.5,000/-, a memento, certificate and a STEM kit. She has been selected to participate in the National Camp to be held in Chandigarh on 16th -17th May, 2020.

Anish Raj, of class VII, won 5 medals (3 Gold, 1 Silver and 1 Bronze) in the prestigious Flanders European World Speed Skating Championship held in Oostende, Belgium from August 9-11, 2019.

Ms. Prisha Rai of class VII has shown remarkable excellence in Karate. She was conferred upon the Dan Black Belt in Karate. She was awarded one Silver and one Bronze medal in 7th International Karate Championship held at Noida, Uttar Pradesh. She also received a Silver Medal in National Level Karate Championship held at New Delhi. She was honored with two Gold medals and one silver medal in District Level Karate Championship

Sukhman Singh, of class VIII, secured first position in the 97th Junior Golf Tournament played at Jaypee Greens, Greater Noida on June 28th, 2019. He also bagged first position in All India Mid-Amateur Golf Tournament held in Bengaluru from July 22-26, 2019.

Miss Harshita Rawtani, student of Amity International School Noida, Class 8-D, participated in Ice Figure Skating Training and Selection Camp conducted by Ice Skating Association of India at Gurugram from 17th to 25th June’19. She recently had undergone Ice Skating Training Camp at Kranevo, Bulgaria from 18th May’19 to 1st June’19 also. Harshita is now selected to participate in the following International Competitions – Asian Open Figure Skating Trophy in China and International Ice Dance Dev.
